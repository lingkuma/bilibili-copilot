Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Bilibili Copilot is a browser extension that summarizes Bilibili video subtitles with an AI provider selected and configured by the user. This Privacy Policy explains what data the extension handles, how that data is used, and when it may be shared.

Data We Handle

The extension may process the following data when you use its features:

  • Bilibili video information, including video title, URL, BV ID, AV ID, CID, and page number.
  • Subtitles and subtitle metadata from the current Bilibili video.
  • AI prompts, summaries, chat questions, chat answers, and generated Markdown content.
  • Extension settings, including your selected language, prompt templates, AI provider endpoint, model name, and API key.
  • Optional sharing settings that you configure, such as Telegraph, Cloudinary, or Telegram credentials and destination IDs.
  • Optional local summary and chat history stored in your browser.

How We Use Data

We use data only to provide and improve the extension's user-facing features:

  • To detect the current Bilibili video and fetch available subtitles.
  • To send subtitles, video context, prompts, and questions to the AI provider you configure so the provider can generate summaries or answers.
  • To save your extension settings locally so you do not need to re-enter them.
  • To keep optional local history so you can revisit previous summaries and conversations.
  • To publish or send content through optional sharing integrations only when you choose to use those features or enable the related setting.

Local Storage

Bilibili Copilot stores settings in Chrome extension storage and may store summary history in IndexedDB in your browser. This data remains on your device unless you choose to send content to an AI provider or an optional sharing service.

Third-Party Services

The extension does not run a separate Bilibili Copilot server for collecting user data. Depending on the features you use, data may be sent directly from your browser to these third parties:

  • Bilibili, to retrieve video and subtitle data for the current video.
  • Your configured OpenAI-compatible AI provider, to generate summaries and answers.
  • Telegraph, if you choose to publish a shared summary page.
  • Cloudinary, if you choose to upload images for a shared page.
  • Telegram, if you choose to send a summary through a Telegram bot.

These services process data under their own terms and privacy policies. You should review the policies of any AI provider or sharing service before configuring it in the extension.

No Sale or Advertising Use

We do not sell user data. We do not transfer user data to advertising platforms, data brokers, or information resellers. We do not use user data for personalized advertising, credit-worthiness, or lending purposes.

Limited Use Statement

The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Human Access

We do not have routine human access to your local settings, API keys, subtitles, summaries, or history. If you voluntarily send information to us for support, we will use it only to respond to your request, investigate issues, or comply with applicable law.

Security

The extension uses HTTPS endpoints for supported network requests. You are responsible for protecting API keys and access tokens that you configure in the extension. Do not share your keys with people you do not trust.

Retention and Deletion

Locally stored settings and history remain in your browser until you delete them, reset the extension, clear browser storage, or uninstall the extension. Data sent to third-party providers is retained according to those providers' policies.

Children's Privacy

Bilibili Copilot is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the extension changes or when legal, security, or Chrome Web Store requirements change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.

Contact

For privacy questions, please open an issue at github.com/lingkuma/bilibili-copilot.
